Lamborghini

In the town of Nurburg is a race track called The Nurburgring. It comprises of huge crests, blind corners and an overall extremely challenging layout unlike any other racetrack where you have vision of what's ahead. This means that cars at the Nurburgring are pushed harder, on worse surfaces and in a larger variety of conditions than at an ordinary track. 'Like no other track in the world, it is the yardstick for the overall performance of a sports car, and relentlessly exposes weaknesses in individual components of the vehicle.
